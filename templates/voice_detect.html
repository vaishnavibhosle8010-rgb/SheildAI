📞 Common Phone Scam Scripts

  • "RBI calling" — RBI never calls individuals directly
  • "Your Aadhaar is linked to crime" — This is pure intimidation fraud
  • "Police will arrest you in 2 hours" — Fake arrest threats to extort money
  • "Your KYC is expired, share OTP" — Banks update KYC at branches, not by phone
  • "You won a prize, pay processing fee" — You never need to pay to receive prizes
