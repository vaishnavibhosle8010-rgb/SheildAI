🛡 Quick Safety Tips

  • Always check the exact spelling of the domain (e.g., sbiindia.com is fake)
  • Legitimate banks use .com, .co.in or .gov.in — never .tk, .ml, .xyz
  • Look for HTTPS (padlock icon) but know HTTPS alone doesn't mean safe
  • Never click links sent via SMS from unknown numbers
