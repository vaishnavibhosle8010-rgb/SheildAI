🛡 Remember These Rules

  • No bank or government agency will EVER ask for your OTP via SMS or call
  • Messages creating extreme urgency ("within 24 hours", "last chance") are almost always scams
  • You cannot win a lottery you did not enter
  • Government agencies send official letters, not WhatsApp messages
  • If in doubt, call the official helpline number of the organization
