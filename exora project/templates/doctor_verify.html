⚠ Common Medical Scams in India

  • Fake Online Consultations: Fraudsters pose as AIIMS/Apollo doctors on WhatsApp
  • Advance Fee Fraud: Asking for fees before showing credentials or treating you
  • Fake Cancer/Disease Treatment: Claiming miracle cures and asking large payments
  • Impersonation: Using names of real famous doctors to gain trust
  • Always verify using the official MCI website or by calling the hospital directly
